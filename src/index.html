About the Project

The Global Table is more than just dinner — it's a community where expats, digital nomads, and locals come together to share stories, cultures, and authentic homemade cuisine.

Every weekend, we transform our space into a culinary journey to a different country. Experience the warmth of home cooking and the joy of meaningful connections.
