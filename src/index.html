About the Project

The Global Table is more than just dinner — it's a community where expats, digital nomads, and locals come together to share stories, cultures, and authentic homemade cuisine.

Every weekend, we transform our space into a culinary journey to a different country. Experience the warmth of home cooking and the joy of meaningful connections. Forget lonely dinners at restaurants. Here, you'll leave with new friends and unforgettable memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dinner cost?

Price varies depending on the cuisine and menu, typically ranging from 1,200 to 1,800 THB per person. Drinks are not included but can be purchased separately.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We have a special field in the booking form where you can specify any allergies or dietary preferences. We'll do our best to adapt the menu for you.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. Later cancellations are non-refundable, but you can transfer your spot to a friend.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely! Our dinners are designed for solo guests to meet new people. Most of our attendees come alone and leave with new friends.

Is alcohol included?

Alcohol is not included in the price, but we offer a selection of wines and beers available for purchase at the event.

Where exactly in Phuket do you host the dinners?

We host dinners in private venues near Kata Beach. The exact address is provided after booking confirmation for privacy and security.
